Reason for Website Creation

2021-07-2
#thoughts 

This website was created mainly to showcase my personal projects and findings and experiences. Writing about what I've done helps me in collecting the entire jargon of information and convey it in an understandable and sequential manner. It helps my writing skills, makes sure website maintenance skills are never out of touch, an extended resume to recruiters and curious minds by showcasing what I know, what I can do, my methodology and the way I think.

So, this website will have mostly technical posts.

Already tried the wordpress blog writing [in a private manner in the beginning] and found out I can write and manage a website. But felt that blog writing feels too chaotic because current situation changes way too fast too often. After positive results from the experiment, I started understanding website building from scratch. So, a website to showcase projects instead of nearly daily writing felt better.

Thought about making a step-by-step guide to freshers and people who are now coming into cybersecurity, but [This isn't a guide of InfoSec, not even trying.. there are many who are much experienced and are better at providing advice out there. I'm not giving out links because they are already out there for free in the wild.] many have already written so much on their websites and videos and the same points are repeated over and over in each of them. It's non-technical and just makes the noobs waste time before diving into the good stuff.

Hopefully this is the only post with the tag: #thoughts
and focus on the game
